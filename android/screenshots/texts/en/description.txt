Do you own Frigate NVR server to store your camera events? This unofficial app will help you to browse events with viewing clips and manage them.

Features:
* Live preview of Frigate NVR cameras
* List of events with filtering with cameras, labels and zones
* Preview of snapshots and clips with zoom
* Removing and retaining events
* Preview of last event from the cameras
* Storage and system info
* Logs of the server
